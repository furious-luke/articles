Killing Time

API Performance and Cluedo

Luke Hodkinson
CTO
Uptick

Hello everyone, thank you all for having me this evening.

This talk is about two things very dear to me: excessively worrying about software performance, and the classic 1940s murder mystery boardgame, Cluedo. At Uptick we found that one of our products had developed a severe performance issue for one of its endpoints. I ended up digging into the supporting Django libraries we use and found some pretty interesting/alarming results. So I'll be talking a bit about our products, the libraries we use, and the tool leveraged to dig into the performance issue.

The marriage of Cluedo with this talk comes primarily in the form of a little game we'll all play about half way in. I'll be asking you to have a guess about what you think the cause of our performance issue was, and the closest guess will be taking away this copy of Cluedo provided by CommonCode. So thank you very much to CommonCode.
