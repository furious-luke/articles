So, what is this talk all about? Well, it's about two things very dear to me: an over-obsession with software performance, and the classic 1940s murder mystery boardgame, Cluedo.

Yes, a talk about the dieing art of performance analysis. At Uptick one of our API endpoints had one foot in the grave. With performance so bad, we were crying bloody murder. When it comes to performance analysis one can't just take a wild stab in the dark, no, we brought to bear a more sophisticated weapon of choice. I'll try and keep this talk short and sharp, so I'll stop killing time and just get on with it.
