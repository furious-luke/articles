Killing Time

API Performance and Cluedo

Luke Hodkinson
CTO
Uptick

Hello everyone, thank you all for having me this evening, especially while I'm dressed as Cololnel Mustard. There is really something about holding a pipe while delivering a talk, you must try it.

This talk is about two things very dear to me: excessively worrying about software performance, and the classic 1940s murder mystery boardgame, Cluedo. At Uptick we found that one of our products had developed a severe performance issue. After some digging around in the supporting Django libraries we use, I found some pretty interesting/alarming results. So I'll be talking a bit about our products, the libraries we use, and the tool leveraged to dig into the performance issue.

The marriage of Cluedo with this talk, in addition to my clothing, comes in the form of a little game we'll all play about half way in. I'll be asking you to have a guess about what you think the cause of our performance issue was, and we'll be giving away a prize to the closest guess. That prize is this here copy of Cluedo, provided by CommonCode, so thanks to CommonCode, and in particular Bec for doing the legwork!
