Now, at the beginning, this use of DRF, and DRF-JSONAPI worked very well indeed. Our Task endpoint performed well enough, taking approximately 400ms for larger queries, but generally was much faster on average.

However over time, as we added more connections to our Task model, and we added more fields, this began to slow down, and we said to ourselves, “we should look in to that one day”.

And then we noticed it slow down even more, and again our sentiment was “one day, we'll get to that when we have time”. It wasn't until some of these task queries were taking up to four seconds that we decided that "that day was today".
